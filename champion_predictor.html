Methodology
Each matchup uses a blended head-to-head Poisson model: a team's expected goals in a specific game is the average of its own attack rate (xG scored/game) and the opponent's defensive weakness (xG conceded/game). A draw is resolved as a coin flip (extra time / penalties). Each matchup is simulated 10,000 times.

Champion probability = P(win Semi Final) × P(win the Final | reaching it) - the Final term is weighted across both possible opponents by their own chance of getting there.

France's, Spain's, Argentina's and England's xG-scored and xG-conceded rates are now both computed directly from their exact match-by-match figures across the tournament (6 matches each, 48 data points total). No tournament-level estimates remain in this model - every rate is sourced from actual match data. Last updated July 12, 2026.
